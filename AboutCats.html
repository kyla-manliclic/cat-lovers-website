About Cats

Cats are one of the most popular pets around the world, known for their playful nature, independence, and mysterious charm.
These furry friends have been companions to humans for thousands of years.
Here are some interesting facts about cats:

  • Cats are obligate carnivores, meaning they require a diet high in animal-based proteins.
  • They have sharp retractable claws that they use for climbing and hunting.
  • Cats have excellent night vision, allowing them to see in light levels six times lower than what a human needs.
  • On average, cats sleep between 12 to 16 hours a day, making them expert nap-takers!
  • Cats communicate with their owners using a variety of vocalizations like meowing, purring, and hissing.
  • Each cat has a unique noseprint, much like a human fingerprint!
  • They have an extraordinary sense of balance, which is why they can often land on their feet from high places.

Cats are not only wonderful companions but also fascinating creatures. Whether you’re a cat owner or just a fan of these independent animals, there’s always something new to learn about them!
